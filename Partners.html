[edit] Australian Defence Simulation Office

The Australian Defence Simulation Office (ADSO) is in active participant and supporter of the baseline release of Portico as part of its Defence Synthetic Environment and Training in a Box Initiatives.

[edit] Calytrix Technologies

Calytrix Technologies actively supports Portico by using the Portico RTI on deliverable projects and developing a Portico plugin for Calytrix' LVC Game software product.
