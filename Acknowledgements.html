The Portico Project and its core development team would like to thank the following people/companies for their support through both development funding and the offering of free services and licenses:

[edit] Calytrix Technologies

Calytrix Technologies are the primary financial supporter of the Portico open source RTI. Calytrix employ the Portico lead developers and provide time for them to contribute to the project to ensure that the simulation community has an open and freely available RTI implementation to meet its needs to and lower barriers to access. Calytrix also provide commercial support for Portico (email sales@calytrix.com for information).

[edit] Corporate Development Funding

Throughout its life, Portico has received additional commercial funding for various commissioned pieces of work such as the introduction of support for new HLA interfaces or the expansion of supported platforms. The Portico team would like to thank these companies for their assistance and support:

[edit] Atlassian

Jira is central to our development process, and thanks to the generosity of Atlassian, we are able to use this amazing piece of software for free. In return, give you some information about them:



"Atlassian supports our efforts by contributing their bug tracking and issue tracking application, JIRA, to our project.

Atlassian is an innovative Australian software company providing enterprise software solutions to the world's leading organisations. Atlassian's leading software product, JIRA is a bug tracking and issue tracking application developed to track and manage the issues and bugs that emerge during a project. JIRA customers include departments from NASA, Boeing, Cisco, JP Morgan, 3M, BP, Sony and more. Try it for yourself today."
